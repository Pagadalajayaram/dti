About the Application

Our application is designed to help find missing persons quickly and efficiently by connecting families, the public, and authorities through a real-time alert system.

How It Works:

  1. Step 1: Families report a missing person through the app with necessary details (photo, last seen location, etc).
  2. Step 2: The system sends real-time notifications to users in nearby locations.
  3. Step 3: Users receive alerts and can view the profile of the missing person.
  4. Step 4: If users spot the person, they can report the sighting with live location updates.
  5. Step 5: Upon successful verification and location of the missing person, users may receive a reward as appreciation.
